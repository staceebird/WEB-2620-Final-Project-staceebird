French Braid


ITEMS NEEDED: brush/comb and elastics

TIME: 15-20 Minutes

SKILL LEVEL: Medium

STEP BY STEP GUIDE

Step 1: Take a section of hair and part it into 3 equal sections.

Step 2: Take the left strand and cross it over and into the middle.

Step 3: Take the right strand and bring it over and into the middle.

Step 4: Now pick up a small section of hair next to the left strand.

Step 5: Add the hair you just picked up to the left strand.

Step 6: Cross the left strand over and into the middle.

Step 7: So pick up a new section of hair next to the right strand.

Step 8: Add the hair you just picked up to the right strand.

Step 9: Now cross the right strand over and into the middle.

Step 10: Pick up some hair next to the left strand and add it to the left strand. Then cross the left strand over and into the middle.

Step 11: Pick up some hair next to the right strand and add it to the right strand. Then cross the right strand over and into the middle.

Step 12: Repeat steps 10-11 until you reach the nape of the neck, then braid the hair like a normal braid.
