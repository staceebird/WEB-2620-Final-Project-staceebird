Fishtail Braid


ITEMS NEEDED: brush/comb and elastics

TIME: 15-20 Minutes

SKILL LEVEL: Medium

STEP BY STEP GUIDE

Step 1: Take a small section of hair at the top of your head and split the section into 2 equal sections

Step 2: Grab a small section of hair from the right strand

Step 3: Then cross it under and add it to the left strand.

Step 4: Grab a small section of hair from the left strand

Step 5: Then cross it under and add it to the right strand.

Step 6: Again grab a small section of hair from the right strand and some new hair from the right side

Step 7: Cross the small section and the new hair under and add it to the left strand.

Step 8: Then grab a small section of hair from the left strand and some new hair from the left side.

Step 9: Take the small section and the new hair under it and add it to the right strand.

Step 10: Repeat steps 6-9 until there is no more hair to add to the braid.

Step 11: With the rest of the hair, braid a reversed fishtail braid until you reach the end of the hair.
