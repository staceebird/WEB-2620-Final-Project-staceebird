Hello, my name is Stacee Bird! I am 21 years old and currently living in Utah. I have not always liked doing hair because I thought I wasn’t good at it but in reality, I was scared that I couldn’t do it. I want to help others learn to feel comfortable fixing their hair. I started becoming more adventurous or comfortable with my hair when I was 16. I was tired of putting my hair in a ponytail all the time, I wanted to do more. I would watch YouTube videos and after a few years, I started to figure out my own tricks when it came to fixing hair. I am always learning new tricks and styles so I am excited to share these hairstyles with you. When fixing hair I don’t want you to think, I can’t do this...instead, tell yourself “I got this!” Let’s get started and have some fun!
