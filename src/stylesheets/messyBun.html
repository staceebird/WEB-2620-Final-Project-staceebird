Messy Bun


ITEMS NEEDED: brush/comb, hair tie/elastics, and bobby pins

TIME: 5-10 Minutes

SKILL LEVEL: Easy

STEP BY STEP GUIDE

Step 1: Put your head down.

Step 2: Grab all your hair to the top center of your head.

Step 3: Hold your hair in place and bring your head back up

Step 4: Wrap your hair once with your hair tie as if you are doing a high ponytail.

Step 5: Pull your hair through the hair tie again but only a quarter of the way.

Step 6: Have the hair tie go over the little hump once to secure it more in place.

Step 7: The hair that was left out of the hump, tuck into the back by creating a loop and sticking it behind the hair tie.

Step 8: Grab a bobby pin. You will grab a small section from the top left side and then put the bobby pin towards the center of the bun with the hair you grabbed from the left.

Step 9: Do step 8 but on the right side.

Step 10: Grab a small section of hair from the top middle and bring it towards the front. Once again, bobby pin that section towards the center of the bun.

Step 11: Pull out some hair around your ears and you are done.
