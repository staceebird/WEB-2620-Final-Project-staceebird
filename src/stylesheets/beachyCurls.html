Beachy Curls


ITEMS NEEDED: brush/comb, large clips, 1 ¼ inch curler, and hair straightener

TIME: 20-30 Minutes

SKILL LEVEL: Easy

STEP BY STEP GUIDE

Step 1: Separate your hair horizontally, you will want to have an inch of hair thickness on the bottom. Clip the top section.

Step 2: Split the bottom section in half. One section over your left shoulder and the other on the right shoulder.

Step 3: Grab an inch width of hair closest to your face. Wrap the hair (away from your face) around the barrel. Leave about 1 ½ inch off of the heat.

Step 4: Let go of the hair after 10-20 seconds on the curler.

Step 5: Grab another inch of hair and repeat steps 3 and 4 but this time you will alternate the direction of the curl. Start with away from the face and then towards the face.

Step 6: Once the section is finished take out the hair from the top section and separate again.

Step 7: Repeat steps 2-6 until hair is all finished.

Step 8: Now that the hair is curled, grab your straightener and straighten your ends.

Step 9: Let your hair cool down

Step 10: Once cooled, flip your head down and run your fingers through the curls

Step 11: Bring your head back up and now you have beachy curls.
